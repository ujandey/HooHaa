What Can HooHaa Do?

Discover the power of HooHaa, your voice-powered AI assistant! Below are examples of what you can ask HooHaa to make your day easier and more fun. Just say "Hey HooHaa" followed by your command!

Everyday Tasks

  • Set reminders with "Hey HooHaa, remind me to call Mom at 5 PM"
  • Create notes with "Hey HooHaa, note that I need to buy milk"
  • Add items to lists with "Hey HooHaa, add eggs to my grocery list"

Information & Assistance

  • Get quick facts with "Hey HooHaa, what’s the weather like today?"
  • Perform calculations with "Hey HooHaa, what’s 25% of 80?"
  • Translate phrases with "Hey HooHaa, how do you say 'hello' in French?"

Personalization & Fun

  • Personalize responses with "Hey HooHaa, tell me a joke"
  • Play games with "Hey HooHaa, flip a coin"
  • Get creative ideas with "Hey HooHaa, suggest a dinner recipe"

Smart Integration (Future Updates)

  • Control smart devices with "Hey HooHaa, turn off the living room lights" (coming soon)
  • Schedule events with "Hey HooHaa, schedule a meeting at 2 PM tomorrow" (coming soon)

Want to explore more? Try asking "Hey HooHaa, what can you do?" while using the assistant page!
